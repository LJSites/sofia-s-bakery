Since the neighborhood was hungry

"A low-key corner of Inglewood where every morning smells like something wonderful."

Sofia's Bakery has been the neighborhood's morning ritual for years. We bake our breads and pastries fresh daily, pull espresso shots that beat the chain up the street, blend real-fruit smoothies, and keep the doors open until you're ready to call it a day. Cafeteria-style, no fuss — just good food and friendlier people.
